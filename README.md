# Developer Audience Playbook

A practical guide to marketing for developer audiences — built from real experience in B2B IT and EdTech, and honest observation of what works (and what developers immediately see through).

This isn't a generic "how to do developer marketing" post. It's a working framework: how to think about developer trust, how to build messaging that doesn't insult their intelligence, and how to make marketing decisions when your audience is fundamentally skeptical of marketing.

---

## Why this exists

I've spent 17 years marketing technical products to professional audiences — IT certifications, B2B SaaS, developer tools. I've made the mistakes that most people make when marketing to developers, and I've figured out what actually works.

The biggest mistake: treating developers like a harder version of a general B2B audience. They're not. They're a completely different communication challenge.
